Introduction

Digg participates in the Large Scale Pilots for the European Digital Identity Wallet (EUDIW), which aim to provide a secure, user-centric, and interoperable digital identity solution across the EU.

As part of this work, Digg contributes by:

  • Testing and providing feedback on the European Commission's open-source reference implementation of the EUDI Wallet, developed to support Member States and stakeholders in building their own wallets.
  • Enabling testing of issuing a Swedish Person Identification Data (PID) to wallets

We also closely follow the latest specifications outlined in the EUDIW Architecture and Reference Framework.

What can I test in the apps?

You can apply for a PID from Digg either by scanning a QR-code for a credential offer or selecting a PID from the preconfigured list you can find in the app. You can also use your newly acquired PID and have it verified on Diggs Verifier either by selective disclosure or by supplying all available data.
